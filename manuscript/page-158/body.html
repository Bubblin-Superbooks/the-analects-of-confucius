Xxxiv.

Wei-shang Mau said to Confucius, 'Ch'iu, how is it that you keep roosting about? Is it not that you are an insinuating talker?' Confucius said, 'I do not dare to play the part of such a talker, but I hate obstinacy.'

Xxxv.

The Master said, 'A horse is called a ch'i, not because of its strength, but because of its other good qualities.'

Xxxvi.

Some one said, 'What do you say concerning the principle that injury should be recompensed with kindness?' The Master said, 'With what then will you recompense kindness? 'Recompense injury with justice, and recompense kindness with kindness.'
