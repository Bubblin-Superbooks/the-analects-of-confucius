Book Xviii. Wei Tsze

I.

The Viscount of Wei withdrew from the court. The Viscount of Chi became a slave to Chau. Pi-kan remonstrated with him and died. Confucius said, 'The Yin dynasty possessed these three men of virtue.'

Ii.

Hui of Liu-hsia being chief criminal judge, was thrice dismissed from his office. Some one said to him, 'Is it not yet time for you, sir, to leave this?' He replied, 'Serving men in an upright way, where shall I go to, and not experience such a thrice- repeated

dismissal? If I choose to serve men in a crooked way, what necessity is there for me to leave the country of my parents?'
