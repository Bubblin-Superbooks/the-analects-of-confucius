Master said, 'You are a utensil.' 'What utensil?' 'A gemmed sacrificial utensil.'

Iv.

Some one said, 'Yung is truly virtuous, but he is not ready with his tongue.' The Master said, 'What is the good of being ready with the tongue? They who encounter men with smartnesses of speech for the most part procure themselves hatred. I know not whether he be truly virtuous, but why should he show readiness of the tongue?'

V.

The Master was wishing Ch'i-tiao K'ai to enter on official employment. He replied, 'I am not yet able to rest in the assurance of THIS.' The Master was pleased.
